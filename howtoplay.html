üéØ Game Overview

The Imposter Game (also known as Undercover or The Chameleon) is a social deduction word game where most players receive the same secret word, but one or more imposters receive a different role. The goal is for the crew to identify the imposter(s) while the imposter(s) try to blend in without knowing the secret word!

üéÆ Setup

  • Select Categories: Choose one or more word categories (Food, Animals, School & Learning, etc.)
  • Imposter Hint: Toggle ON to give imposters a helpful hint about the secret word
  • Chaos Mode: Enable for random imposters (0 to all players) and flexible voting
  • Add Players: Enter names for all players (minimum 3 players required)

üé¥ Role Assignment

After entering all player names, each player will tap their name on the device to privately reveal their role:

  • Crew Members: See the secret word (e.g., "Pizza")
  • Imposter(s): See "IMPOSTER!" message
  • If hints are enabled, imposters get a clue (e.g., "It starts with P" or "It has 5 letters")
  • One random player is marked as the starting player ‚≠ê

After discussion rounds, players vote for who they think is the imposter:

  • Each player votes for ONE suspect
  • Player(s) with the most votes are suspected imposters

  • Each player can vote for multiple suspects (up to 3)
  • Number of imposters (0 to all players) is randomly selected and unknown!
  • Adds extra unpredictability and challenge

üïµÔ∏è Imposter Wins: If the imposter(s) avoided detection, they get 1 point each

üí° Strategy Tips

For Crew Members:

  • Give specific but not too obvious clues
  • Listen for vague or mismatched clues from others
  • Don't reveal the exact word!
  • Work together to spot suspicious behavior

For Imposters:

  • Listen to other clues before giving yours if possible
  • Give generic clues that could apply to many words
  • Use your hint wisely to make educated guesses
  • Act confident even when you're unsure!

üé≤ About Chaos Mode

Chaos Mode adds an exciting twist to the game:

  • Random Imposters: The game randomly selects 0 to all players as imposters (no one knows how many!)
  • Flexible Voting: Players can vote for multiple suspects (up to 3)
  • More Deception: Imposters might not even know if they have allies
  • Greater Challenge: Makes the game more unpredictable and fun!
